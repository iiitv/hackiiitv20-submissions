HEAD RELATED

1)Double vision
2)Seizures                            
3)Mental confusion                     
4)Loss of memory
5)Paralysis


TYPE 2
1)Short-term memory loss                     
2)Impaired judgment                       
3)Language difficulties 
4)Recognition issues 
5)Difficulty in walking

TYPE 3                            
1)Sudden fear                                                                      
2)Uncontrolled movement of body parts       
3)Confusion 
4)Loss of consciousness

TYPE 4
1)nausea                               
2)severe headache 
3)partial paralysis                  
4)drowsiness 
5)epileptic seizures 
6)discharge of pus from the ear or nose 

TYPE 5
1)Headache                                                         
2)Paralysis                             
3)Partial or complete loss of vision
4)Loss of memory 
5)Inability to walk and coma
